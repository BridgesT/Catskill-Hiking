The Catskill Mountains feature some of the most beautiful ranges in New York. They feature spanning landscapes and trails ranging from beginner to challenging. Whether you want to take a stroll to appreciate nature or push yourself physically and mentally the Catskills have you covered. Best of all, it's all free! Just park and hit the trails. I want to use this page to display my photography so others can appreciate nature and to inspire others to do healthy activities.
